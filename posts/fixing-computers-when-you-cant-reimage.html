Occam's Razor - "The simplest answer is usually the best one"
The problem-solving principle known as Occam's Razor can be used in many ways of life including working in IT. In most cases if someone has a computer issue and its not hardware related the simplest way to fix the problem is to reimage the machine but in some cases you will have critical information stored on the drive and you cannot reimage as you will lose this information whether it be helping with your Grandma's computer or a manager who refuses to store files on the server. So what do we do when we can't reimage the computer? There is currently 2 utilities I use, the first is a script "Tron" whichÂ automatically runs through and removes temporary files, removes unnecessary software, runs multiple virus scans, performs updates and optimizes the drive. The second is a bootable disc called "Hiren's BootCD" which has a large number of tools including Memtest86+ for testing if there is an issue with your RAM and another great tool for resetting local Windows passwords. Downloads:
  • Tron
  • Hiren's BootCD
